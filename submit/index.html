Call For Submissions

Join The Widening Gyre

Do you desire an outlet for creativity? Do you revere print media? Do you reject the mixing of AI and art? Do you keep a hand-written journal with little notes that could blossom into full essays? Do you want your work to be read by a small but mighty group of people, in digital and in print form?

We are accepting both one-off pieces and writers and artists interested in contributing on a semi-consistent and/or consistent basis. If you want to submit once — that’s great! If you want to make this part of your routine of life, we welcome you to persist in the eye of the storm with us.

Currently accepting

• Essays• Reviews • Journalism• Illustrations • Theory• Art prints • Prose• Digital art • Poetry• Photography • Sports writing• Other!

Any and all pitches are encouraged. Send them to thewideninggyremag@gmail.com, or reach us by mail at 8282 Meadowhaven Dr., Sacramento, CA 95828.

We encourage feedback! If you would like to send us a message, you can email us at thewideninggyremag@gmail.com or reach us via mail at 8282 Meadowhaven Dr. Sacramento, CA 95828. You can also fill out the feedback form: bit.ly/thewideninggyrefeedback.
